About us

Milele africa Institute of Development of Nairobi is educational foundation with over five years of experience in delivering effective and efficient training at Diploma and Certificate levels in areas such as Engineering ICT, Business and Social Sciences. Our programs are designed to foster professional and personal development. The up-to-date curricula for the various programs offered at the College are designed to offer relevance in the rapidly changing world and foster the qualities of imagination and independence of thought.
